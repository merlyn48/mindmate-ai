🔒 Privacy Policy

How MindMate AI handles your information — clearly and honestly.

At MindMate AI, your privacy is a core value — not an afterthought. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, how it's used, and the control you have over it. We believe you deserve a clear answer, not legal jargon.

Last updated: May 2025
What We Collect

📋 Account Information

When you sign in with Firebase Authentication, we collect your email address and a display name (if provided). This is used only to identify your account session and personalise your experience.

💬 Chat Messages

Messages you send to MindMate AI are transmitted to our backend (Netlify Functions) and forwarded to the Claude AI API to generate a response. Chat messages are not stored on any server — they exist only for the duration of the request.

📓 Journal Entries & Mood Data

Journal entries and mood logs are saved entirely in your browser's Local Storage. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

What We Never Do
  • Sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties
  • Store your chat conversations on our servers
  • Share your journal entries or mood data with anyone
  • Use your data to train AI models
  • Track you across other websites or apps
  • Send you unsolicited marketing emails
Third-Party Services

MindMate AI uses a small number of trusted third-party services to function:

  • Firebase (Google) — handles user authentication (sign in / sign up). Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — processes your chat messages to generate AI responses. Messages are subject to Anthropic's data usage policy.
  • Netlify — hosts the application and serverless functions. Standard access logs may be collected by Netlify.
💡 No personally identifiable information is shared with these services beyond what is strictly necessary for them to function.
Your Rights & Controls

You are in control of your data. Here's what you can do at any time:

🗑️ Delete Your Data Clear your journal and mood history anytime from your Profile settings or by clearing browser Local Storage.
🔓 Sign Out Logging out ends your session and removes your auth token from the browser.
🚫 Stop Using the App You can stop using MindMate at any time. No subscriptions, no commitments.
📩 Contact Us Have questions about your data? Reach out via the Contact page and we'll respond.
Educational Project Notice

MindMate AI was developed as an educational project to explore how AI can support student mental wellness. It is not a commercial product and is not operated by a registered healthcare provider. While we take privacy seriously, this application has not undergone formal legal compliance review (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR audit).

We encourage users not to share highly sensitive personal information through the platform beyond what feels comfortable for a supportive chat tool.

Questions about this policy? We're happy to clarify anything. Reach us via the Contact page. This policy may be updated occasionally — the "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
