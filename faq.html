Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things students ask most.

About the AI
Is MindMate powered by real AI?
Yes — MindMate uses the Claude AI API (by Anthropic) to power conversations. This means it can understand nuanced messages, respond thoughtfully, and adapt to what you're saying rather than relying on rigid scripts or keyword matching.
Can MindMate understand how I'm feeling?
MindMate is designed to be empathetic and context-aware. It picks up on emotional cues in your messages and responds with care. That said, it's an AI — it doesn't truly "feel," but it is built to respond in a way that is supportive, validating, and helpful.
Does MindMate remember past conversations?
Within a single session, MindMate maintains context throughout your conversation. However, chat history is not currently persisted between sessions. Your journal entries are saved locally and are always accessible.
What languages does MindMate support?
MindMate can understand and respond in multiple languages since the underlying Claude model is multilingual. However, the interface itself is currently in English only.
Privacy & Data
Where is my data stored?
Your journal entries, mood logs, and profile settings are stored locally in your browser using Local Storage. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share them. Chat messages are sent to the AI to generate responses but are not stored on any server.
Is my data shared with anyone?
No. MindMate does not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third party. The only external service involved is the AI API used to generate chat responses, which is governed by its own privacy policy.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. Since data is stored locally in your browser, you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's Local Storage, or by using the option in your profile settings. This action is irreversible, so make sure you're ready before proceeding.
Mental Health & Safety
Is MindMate a replacement for therapy?
No — and this is important. MindMate is a supportive tool, not a licensed mental health service. It can help you feel heard, process thoughts, and build healthy habits. But it is not a substitute for professional therapy, counselling, or psychiatric care. If you're struggling seriously, please reach out to a professional.
What if I'm having a crisis or emergency?
If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline in your country right away. MindMate is not equipped to handle emergencies. In India, you can reach iCall at 9152987821 or Vandrevala Foundation at 1860-2662-345 (24/7).
Can students with anxiety or depression use MindMate?
MindMate is designed to be a gentle, supportive space for students dealing with everyday stress, anxiety, and low moods. If you have a diagnosed condition, MindMate can complement your care but should not replace it. Always follow the guidance of your healthcare provider.
Using the App
Is MindMate free to use?
Yes — MindMate AI is currently free to use. It was built as an educational project and is made available at no cost to students.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. MindMate is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets, as well as desktop browsers. No app download is required.
Why isn't the AI responding?
If MindMate isn't responding, try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection. The AI requires an active connection to generate responses. If the issue persists, please use the Contact page to let us know.
