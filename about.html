About MindMate AI

MindMate AI is an AI-powered mental wellness companion built specifically for students — helping you navigate stress, anxiety, academic pressure, and everyday emotional challenges with compassionate, judgment-free support.

💬

Supportive Conversations

Chat with an AI trained to listen and respond with empathy — any time, without waiting for an appointment.

📓

Guided Journaling

Reflect on your day, track your moods, and gain insights into your emotional patterns over time.

🌿

Calm Space

Wind down with ambient sounds and breathing exercises designed to reduce tension and restore focus.

🔒

Private & Secure

Your conversations and journal entries stay in your browser. Nothing is sent to external servers.

🛠 Built With

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JavaScript Firebase Auth Claude AI (Anthropic) Local Storage Web Audio API
⚠️ MindMate AI is a supportive tool, not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a crisis or severe distress, please reach out to a licensed counsellor, therapist, or a mental health helpline in your region.
