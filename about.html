About MindMate AI

A student-first mental wellness companion, built with care.

Your mind deserves support — anytime.

MindMate AI is an AI-powered counselling assistant built to help students navigate the pressures of academic life — from exam stress and burnout to motivation slumps and self-doubt. It's a safe, judgment-free space available whenever you need it.

Our Mission
💬

Always Available

Talk through what's on your mind at 2 AM before an exam or during a lunch break — no appointments, no waiting rooms.

🛡️

Private by Default

Your conversations stay in your browser. We don't collect, sell, or share your personal data with anyone.

🎯

Student-Focused

Built specifically around the challenges students face — deadlines, peer pressure, academic burnout, and self-growth.

🌱

Growth-Oriented

Beyond support, MindMate helps you build healthier habits, reflect through journaling, and find calm every day.

What You Can Do Here
  • 🤖
    AI Chat Support Vent, ask for advice, or just talk. MindMate listens and responds with supportive, evidence-informed guidance tailored to student life.
  • 📓
    Guided Journaling Daily prompts and a private journal to help you process emotions, track moods, and build self-awareness over time.
  • 🌿
    Calm Space Ambient audio, breathing exercises, and a distraction-free environment to help you decompress and reset between sessions.
  • 📊
    Dashboard Insights Get a visual overview of your mood trends and journal activity to better understand your own patterns.
Built With

MindMate AI is an educational project exploring how technology can meaningfully support student wellbeing.

⚡ HTML5 🎨 CSS3 🧩 JavaScript 🤖 Groq AI API 🔥 Firebase Auth

⚠️ Important: MindMate AI is a supportive tool — it is not a licensed mental health service and does not replace professional therapy, counselling, or medical advice. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please reach out to a qualified counsellor, helpline, or emergency services in your region.
